What is YouTube Playlist Duration Calculator?

YouTube Playlist Duration Calculator

This app calculates the total length of a YouTube Playlist. The result can also be translated to other speed options.

How to get YouTube playlist URL?

On App

Play a video on YouTube. Above the comments sections. A share button is visible, click the button and select ‘Copy link’

On Browser

Play a video on YouTube. Copy the link on the URL tab.
